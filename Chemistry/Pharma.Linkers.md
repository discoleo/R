
# Linkers for Pharmaceutical Applications

## Pentacyclic Triterpenes

The pentacyclic skeleton is very rigid, offering a good solution to applications requiring a rigid linker.

Some compounds may show varying degrees of toxicity. However, they may still be useful as components of a targeted drug-system (e.g. in oncology).

### Antibodies Binding to Linker
Furthermore, raising antibodies against such a molecule may be feasible. Having specific antibodies against such a linker may offer additional means to control or monitor the drug system.

Adding 1 or 2 halogen atoms to the triterpene skeleton may increase immunogenicity of such a molecule. Adding iodine may pose a risk of allergenicity in atopic patients. Bromine may substantially increase antigenicity with minimal risk of allergenicity - making it an ideal candidate for this application.
